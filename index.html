01 — The Concept

The Whole
Venue Is the Event

PubKey DC runs 12,000 square feet across Penn Quarter. Every room operates simultaneously. Games on every screen. Gemini's prediction market in the stream overlay. A branded hot dog cart on the main floor. Basketball machines in the back. A beer pong bracket on the venue screen.

One Elite Eight game, five activations, one stream. The prediction market overlay ties every zone together — whether someone's at the bar, in the back room, or watching on stream, they're inside the same market.

📊
Prediction market as live product activation
Coaster QR codes and stream overlay funnel every guest directly into Gemini's prediction market. Product adoption happens inside the event, not after.
🏀
March Madness is a universal entry point
The Elite Eight draws a crowd that isn't already crypto-native. Sports creates the context, prediction markets create the hook.
🎥
Every activation is a clip
Market flips, buzzer beaters, pong finals called on stream. The content schedule below maps each moment to platform and format.
🌭
Gemini branding in every frame
Stream overlay, hot dog cart, coasters, pong table felt, venue screens. The brand is the environment — visible in every wide shot and social post.
